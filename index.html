App Description

Reminix is a revolutionary app designed to help families of Alzheimer’s patients stay connected through personalized, AI-generated content. By turning memories, photos, and answers to simple questions into heartfelt stories, images, and music, Reminix aims to spark recognition and joy. The app provides a safe, private, and emotionally meaningful way to reconnect through memories, offering visual, written, and musical outputs that can be easily shared with loved ones. With Reminix, families can create a lasting bond and cherish the moments that matter most.
